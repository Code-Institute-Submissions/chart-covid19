A Simple Presentation of Daily COVID19 Pandemic Cumulative Cases

Charts for Everyone

A Multi-line chart of Cumulative Cases for counties for the US State of Massachusetts with a date line-selector.
A Choropleth Map of the number of cumulative cases and deaths by US counties for a date selected.

  • Step
    1

    Select Date

    Use the line selector on the chart to select a date
  • Step
    2

    View Map Information

    Hover your mouse over a county to view the cumulative number of cases and deaths
  • Step
    3

    Select Different Date

    Repeat Step 1 and 2

Small Device Usage: Tap on the chart area to move the data selection line
